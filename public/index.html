trntxt.uk (beta)

Presenting trntxt: a data-friendly train times site designed for phones on GPRS.

Usage

Station names can be entered using either their 3 letter station codes, or their name (without spaces).
e.g. 'bristolparkway', 'bristolp' or 'bpw' will get you times for Bristol Parkway.

/from
For example, /bathspa will show the full departure board for Bath Spa

/from/to
For example, /paddington/reading will find trains departing Paddington, calling at Reading

About

trntxt is in beta. There are still a few things to do, such as finding arrival times at the destination, and giving details about rail replacement bus services.

This site is designed to work on any mobile device. There are no images or any other fancy things, so you get the data you need as quickly as possible.
